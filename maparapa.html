Maparapa.com

My thesis project for 2013 Interactive Telecommunication Program of Tisch School of the Arts


It is a web-based projection mapping tool with an emphasis on providing a simple, easy and intuitive projection mapping experience.


Maparapa.com is an opportunity for everyone to give projection mapping a shot. As a web-based application, Maparapa users can upload their own videos, choose from a built-in library of audio-reactive animations, or grab videos straight from the web from sites like YouTube, Vimeo or Vine. Mapawrapa's unique approach to projection mapping, makes it easy for all to activate a space with their media of choice
